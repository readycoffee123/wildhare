WHO WE ARE

It happened virtually overnight. One of those opportunities that presents itself and cannot be denied, as if the forces of the universe came together to ignite a spark, which has been blazing like a wild fire ever since! We owe this adventure in part to our friend Carolyn, who saw in us a potential for her business that none of us could have imagined at the time. And so, in what seemed like the blink of an eye, we began to transform her quaint Coffee shop into our buzzing Bistro. And what a wild ride it’s been! Mostly we believe in you, for inspiring us to excel in our endeavor, and assisting us in the important business of preserving Bemidji’s unique, local flavor. If you haven’t already, we invite you to join us, and hope that your palate will be inspired to return often.
